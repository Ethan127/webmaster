Our Mission

Whether it's because of work, distance, or a global pandemic, accessing a medical professional can be difficult. Here at Gomperta, we believe in convenience and accessibility. With the click of a button, anyone, anywhere can meet with a certified medical professional anytime.

Who Are We?

Gomperta is a telehealth company founded by Siddharth Srikanth, Patrick Dobranowski, Ethan Schnathorst, Kevin Chen, and Justin Sheu. Inspired by leading Dutch physician Dr. Rebecca Gomperts, our mission is to make medical care more accessible to those unable to physically see a doctor, especially during recent global events.
