Why Electric

Reduce Greenhouse Gas Emissions
Electric vehicles produce way fewer emissions as compared to modern-day transportation, therefore, the carbon dioxide emissions are lowered greatly, and factors, such as climate change, become less prominent. Greenhouse gas emissions will not be released from electrically-powered vehicles, thereby, producing a grand result across the entire world. Through the reduction in CO2 emissions, the overall heat stored within the atmosphere will certainly lower, and a national environmental issue could be averted.
Saves Money in the Long Run
Electrically-powered vehicles can save a substantial amount of money, regardless of the household, with much lower fuel costs on average than conventional gas vehicles. The average U.S. household spends nearly one-fifth of its total family expenditures on transportation, therefore, saving on fuel can make a big difference in terms of the average family's budget. Electricity is less expensive than gasoline and EVs are more efficient than gasoline vehicles. Electricity prices are also generally much more stable than gasoline prices.
Typically Safer than Combustion Vehicles
Electric vehicles are typically much safer than combustion vehicles. Here at Tesla, our excellent group of mechanical professionals test our newly developed BEV's to assure that the customer has complete trust over our company. We attempt to provide an extremely safe and beneficial experience to those who are invested within our company's facets, and as we technologically advance, our company attempts to provide products at the absolute best quality.
Requires Less Maintenance
At Tesla, we pride ourselves on creating a company that utilizes principles that are extremely cost-efficient and environmentally-friendly. Our EV's require significantly less maintenance as compared to conventional gas vehicles, particularly as a result of technologically advanced components, and the removal of all transmission fluids. This additionally improves the safety of our vehicle, and has contributed to many previous successes that the company has experienced.
