About Us



Who Are We?

Volta is an electric car company founded by Siddharth Srikanth, Patrick Dobranowski, Ethan Schnatherhorst, Esvaran Selvaganapathy, and Mitchell Kubina. We are inspired by Alessandro Volta, who invented the electric battery and is the pioneer of electricity and power. Our mission is to provide cars that are both environmentally sustainable and enjoyable for the public to enjoy.



Why Go Electric?

Electric cars have been proven to reduce greenhouses gases and air pollutants over the entire course of the vehicle compared to other cars that run on petrol or diesel. Our cars are also economically cheaper to maintain, as you will save up to six times on what is paid for average annual gas prices for a car. From less noise pollution, easier maintenance and economic efficiency, Volta's electric cars are what is best!
