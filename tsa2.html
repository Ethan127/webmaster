Leadership and Community






The Rock Canyon TSA Chapter is also constantly involved in community service projects that truly showcase the leadership qualities of all individuals that contribute to the organization. One of the several events that the chapter has been renowned for contributing to is the Fleece-For-Fighters event, which assists in providing blankets for all retired military personnel that are currently experiencing difficulties in their post-retirement life. The RCHS Chapter has always been one of the top contributors in the state of Colorado, and will continue to extend their prowess into the new decade.
