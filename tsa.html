Rock Canyon's Career and Technical Education


Rock Canyon has an outstanding TSA program, aided by an emphasis on computer, technology, and business classes. Countless awards have been won at the state and national levels. Just last year, Rock Canyon had a team attend nationals in Washington, DC for Engineering Design.



Business

Rock Canyon High School participates in the High School of Business program, preparing students for the professional world. Students gain invaluable leadership skills and business strategies. Students in the final High School of Business class even create their own business and present their experience at the end.

Biotechnology

Taught by Ms. Fordham and Ms. Petri, Rock Canyon's Biotechnology program gives students the toolset needed to pursue independent research, as is done in the second year of Biotechnology. It also teaches high schoolers the science behind cutting-edge breakthroughs and technology in the fields of technology, medicine, and biotechnology. Not only that, but students of the biotechnology program graduate with an experience in lab equipment rivaling that of professionals.

Computer Science

The Computer Science classes at Rock Canyon High School enable students' success in nearly any technical field. In the increasingly computerized world, computer science becomes all the more important.

RCTV

Rock Canyon High School has an award-winning school broadcast class, called RCTV (Rock Canyon Television). Students use their creativity, video-editing, and acting skills to produce a professional, and visually appealing production.

Fire Science

Rock Canyon High School poses a unique opportunity for those interested in firefighting: the Fire Science program. Students learn how to deal with real fires and all of the equipment associated with it.

Engineering

Engineering classes teach students how to think critically and feel comfortable with the engineering process, required for engineering-related careers.
